COX MILL

ACT/SAT STUDY CLUB

The ACT/SAT Study Club at Cox Mill is a newly established club with the goal of improving student test scores. The ACT as well as the SAT are some of, if not the most important tests students will take in their high school career. Our club is made up of several members that have taken these tests and are familiar with the types of questions asked. We want to work together with other students to get their minds ready for the problems they'll face. So if you feel like you need a little help, or even a lot of help, be sure to stop by so we can help you study for a brighter future.
